The Kaizen Story

I founded Kaizen Research and Training in 2009 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a continuous improvement training ground for professionals seeking to reach their career pinnacle. The name "Kaizen" - a Japanese philosophy meaning "continuous improvement" - reflects my core belief that excellence is not a destination but a journey of constant growth and refinement.

What started as a mentoring program for marketing students and professionals has evolved into a comprehensive career coaching practice that has transformed over 500 careers. Through years of working with both successful leaders and those facing career challenges, I've identified the key patterns and "X-factors" that distinguish those who accelerate their careers from those who plateau.

My approach combines strategic career planning with the development of what I call "career capital" - the combination of skills, relationships, and reputation that creates sustainable professional success. I believe that everyone has unique strengths that, when properly identified and leveraged, can become the foundation for extraordinary career achievement.
